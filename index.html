Ph.D. Student in Mechanical Engineering @ UCLA

About

I am a Ph.D. student in mechanical engineering at UCLA advised by Prof. Joseph Teran. Before entering graduate school, I received my Bachelor’s degree in mechanical engineering from Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute in 2017.

Research

My research interests are in physics-based animations in computer graphics and computational mechanics, including various simulations of deformable solids, fluids, and thermomechanical effects.

Outside of school, I interned at Amazon in 2022, where I worked on cloth simulations and differentiable physics engine. Earlier in 2021, I did an internship at X, where I worked on implementing a new model in Bullet to accelerate the deformable object simulations.
