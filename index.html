about

I am currently an AI Engineer at NetEase Games. Prior to this, I completed my PhD degree in mechanical engineering at UCLA in January 2024, advised by Prof. Joseph Teran. Before entering graduate school, I received my Bachelor’s degree in mechanical engineering from Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute in 2017.

research

While my background is in mechanical engineering (focusing on computational mechanics, to be more specific), my research focuses on physics-based simulations of various substances and phenomena in computer graphics, such as clothes, volumetric deformable solids, fluids, thermomechanical effects, etc.
